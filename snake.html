How to Play Snake Game

  1. Objective: Control the snake to eat the apples and grow longer.
  2. Starting the Game: Click the "Start Game" button to begin. A countdown will appear.
  3. Controlling the Snake:
    • Arrow Right: Move right
    • Arrow Left: Move left
    • Arrow Up: Move up
    • Arrow Down: Move down
    • Spacebar: Pause/Resume game
  4. Game Over: The game ends if the snake hits the walls or itself.
  5. Winning the Game: Fill the entire grid to win!
  6. Scoring: Your score and best score will be displayed.
  7. Pause Feature: Press spacebar to pause and resume the game.
